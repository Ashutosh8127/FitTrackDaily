FitTrack Daily

Privacy Policy · Last updated: March 2026

🔒 Short version: FitTrack Daily stores all your data locally on your own device. We collect nothing. We transmit nothing. There are no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

1. Information We Collect

FitTrack Daily does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

All data you enter — meals, weight, steps, sleep, photos, notes, and settings — is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage using the chrome.storage.local API. This data:

  • Stays on your device only
  • Is never sent to any server or third party
  • Is never used for advertising or analytics

2. Permissions Used

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — To save your daily logs, settings, and diet plan locally on your device
  • alarms — To schedule your daily reminder notification at your chosen time, even when the popup is closed
  • notifications — To show you the daily log reminder and fasting completion alerts

No network or host permissions are requested. The extension cannot access any website or make any internet requests.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Your data is stored in chrome.storage.local on your device. It persists until you:

  • Use the Reset Week button inside the extension
  • Use the extension's import feature to overwrite data
  • Uninstall the extension (Chrome clears local storage on uninstall)
  • Clear your browser data manually

4. Photos

Progress photos you upload are converted to base64 data-URLs and stored locally in chrome.storage.local. They are never uploaded anywhere. The extension warns you when storage is nearing Chrome's 10 MB limit.

5. Export & Import

The Export feature generates a local JSON file downloaded to your computer. The Import feature reads a JSON file from your computer. Neither operation involves any external server or service.

6. Third-Party Services

FitTrack Daily uses no third-party services, SDKs, analytics, or CDNs. All code is self-contained within the extension package.

7. Children's Privacy

FitTrack Daily is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the updated version will be included with new extension releases. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
